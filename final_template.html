This dataset was compiled by Maddie Stelk. It shows the number and real median earnings of total and full-time, year-round workers ages 15+ by sex and female-to-male earnings ratio from 1960 to 2019. The raw data was collected through the Current Population Survey Annual Social and Economic Supplements (CPS ASEC), conducted by the U.S. Census Bureau. The Census Bureau collected this data to evaluate national economic trends and understand their impact on the well-being of households, families, and individuals.
